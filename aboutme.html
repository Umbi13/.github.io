About

Passionate about history and old vintage stuff, he looks at the world and current affairs with great curiosity. He has done many jobs, so different from one another, that allowed him to accumulate a good level of experience which he hopes to make in to work in the next years.

So far, during the years, he got interested in photography, videomaking and writing, coming to the conclusion that storytelling pushed him to look beyond things, to be able to tell them.
